NCG Series · Batch 9: “Structural Problems in the New Testament”

The Gospel of Christ · The Trinity · The Virgin Mary

For centuries, the New Testament has been regarded as the foundation of Christian belief.

Yet fundamental questions have rarely been asked.

Are the Gospels truly the message Jesus preached—

or narratives shaped and reconstructed over time?

If the “Gospel of Christ” once existed,
why is it no longer found in the Bible today?
If the Trinity is not explicitly stated in Scripture,
is it a revealed truth—or a later theological construct?
If Mary had other children,
why has she come to be venerated as the Virgin Mother?
Then we must ask:
What is the New Testament we believe in today actually built upon?
