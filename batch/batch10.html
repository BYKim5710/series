Culmination of the Religious Revolution (Grand Finale)

The Religious Revolution is not meant to destroy religion.

It is a declaration to reclaim the teachings of the sages and return them to humanity.

Many religions did not inherit the teachings proclaimed by the sages. Instead, they distorted them and transformed them into instruments of power.

The Buddhahood, Godhood, and Heavenhood within human beings were erased, and the afterlife became a tool of fear.

Yet the teachings of the Buddha, Jesus, and Si-hyung Choi of Donghak point in the same direction.

“Awaken the inherent essence within all beings, and realize a Spiritual Field of coexistence and co-prosperity on Earth.”

Now we must move beyond distorted doctrines and myths,
and rediscover truth through the laws of nature.
