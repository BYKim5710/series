Dawn of the Religious Revolution — The Question Left by the Fear of Death

Death came too early, and far too cold.

The deaths of family members during childhood left behind questions that would remain for a lifetime.

Where did we come from? What are life and death? Do God and the afterlife truly exist?

In search of those answers, I spent more than 50 years studying religion, philosophy, and science.

“Death is not the end, but only the transmigration of entities — and all beings are connected through one essence, Nosome (無有).”

This realization became the dawn of the Religious Revolution.
Episode 1 reveals why this long journey began.
