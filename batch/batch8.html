But one essential question has rarely been asked:

If we do not even know what Jesus really looked like, how could we recognize him if he were to return?

If we do not know his appearance,
what are we actually recognizing?
And if he returns,
how will we know it is truly him?
Who has the authority
to identify the real Jesus?
