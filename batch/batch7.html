NCG Series – Batch 7: “The Historical Facts of the Old Testament”

The Exodus of Moses & the Empire of David & Solomon

What if the two greatest stories of the Old Testament were never historical events?

For over two millennia, these two narratives have formed the historical backbone of the Old Testament.

But archaeology and historical research raise serious questions about both.

Many scholars now suggest that these accounts may not describe events as they actually happened, but traditions shaped and written centuries later.

So the question remains:
If the historical foundation of the Old Testament is uncertain, what becomes of the theology built upon it?
